Chicken Yakisoba

Description

Yakisoba is a Japanese stir-fried noodle dish. It's made with wheat-based noodles, meat, and vegetables, stir-fried in a sweet and savory sauce.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1⁄4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1⁄8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1⁄4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1⁄4 cup water

Noodles

  • 2 5.6 oz pkg of yakisoba noodles
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage

  1. Follow noodle package instructions to heat and seperate

  2. In a small bowl, combine yakisoba sauce ingredients: soy sauce, sugar, red pepper flakes, vinegar, and water. Whisk to combine.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large saut√© pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add sliced onions and stir-fry for 1 minute. Add cabbage and cook until softened.

  4. Add the prepped yakisoba noodles and toss well.

  5. Pour the yakisoba sauce into the pan and toss to coat the noodles. Stir-fry for 3 to 4 minutes until heated through.

  6. Turn off the heat and add scallions, Serve.
