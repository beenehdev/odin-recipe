Chicken Yakisoba

Description

Yakisoba is a Japanese stir-fried noodle dish. It's made with wheat-based noodles, meat, and vegetables, stir-fried in a sweet and savory sauce.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1⁄4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1f⁄8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1⁄4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1⁄4 cup water

Noodles

  • 2 5.6 oz pkg of yakisoba noodles
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
