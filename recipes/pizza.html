Margherita Pizza

Description

A Margherita pizza is a classic italian-style pizza known for both simplicity and flavor. It's made of a thin crust topped with fresh tomatos, white mozzarella and basil.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 21⁄2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1⁄2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3⁄4 tsp kosher salt
  • 7 oz warm water
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Pizza Sauce

  • 1 cup pureed or crushed tomatos
  • 2-3 fresh garlic cloves
  • 2-3 pinches of kosher salt
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1⁄4 tsp ground black pepper

Toppings

  • 7 oz fresh mozarella
  • 5-6 large basil leaves

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together all purpose flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil, and stir the mixture until the dough just begins to come together.

  2. Scrape dough onto a floured counter top. Knead the dough for three minutes. Dust dough with flour as needed. After, grease a mixing bowl and place dough in bowl.

  3. Cover the bowl with kitchen wrap. Allow dough to rise in a warm, dry area for 2 hours or until dough has doubled in size.

  4. Preheat oven oven to 500Â°F with pizza steel or stone inside.

  5. As oven is preheating, assemble ingredients. In a small bowl, stir together tomatos, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, pepper, and salt. Set aside another small bowl with the cubed mozzarella cheese. Set aside the basil leaves and optional toppings.

  6. Seperate dough into two equal portions. Place dough on a large plate or floured counter top. Cover with plastic wrap, and allow dough to rest for roughly 5-10 minutes.

  7. Stretch or spin ball of pizza dough into a roughly 10-inch circle. Place Dough on pizza peel or similar flat carrying tool.

  8. Brush dough with olive oil. Add tomato sauce while leaving a border on all sides. Add cubed mozzarella, distributed evenly over pizza. Sprinkle basil onto pizza. Gently slide pizza from peel to sheet in oven. Bake for 7-8 minutes.

  9. Repeat with second pizza dough, servce and enjoy!
