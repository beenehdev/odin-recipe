Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken (also known as Murgh Makhani), is a rich and creamy dish featuring marinated chicken cooked in a gravy-like sauce. It's often served with rice or bread, like naan.

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 1.1 lbs boneless chicken
  • 3⁄4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1⁄3 tsp salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1⁄8 tsp turmeric
  • 1⁄2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3⁄4 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1⁄3 cup greek yogurt

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3⁄4 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 to 2 deseeded green chilis
  • 1 to 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1⁄2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1⁄2 tbsp kasuri methi
  • 1 cinnamon
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 4 cloves

Puree

  • 1.2 lbs diced tomatos
  • 28 cashews

Instructions

  1. Prepare chicken marinade ingredients.

  2. Pat dry chicken. Add red chili powder, salt and lemon juice. Mix well, cover and rest for 20 mins.

  3. Later, add ginger garlic paste, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, kasuri methi, turmeric, and greek yogurt. Mix well and cover. Let chicken rest in the marinade for 12 hours minimum, refrigerated.

  4. After finishing chicken marination. Remove from fridge. Prepare sauce ingredients.

  5. Blend puree ingredients (tomatos, cashews) with 1⁄2 cup water into a smooth puree.

  6. Heat butter in a pan. Add cinnamon, cloves, and cardamoms. Once sizzling, add ginger garlic paste and green chilis, then fry on medium heat for 2 minutes.

  7. Turn off heat. Stir in red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and coriander powder.

  8. Pour the puree.

  9. Mix well and bring to boil on medium high heat.

  10. Cover partially and let boil on medium heat. Stir often for about 15 minutes, or until thick.

  11. Add 11⁄2 hot water and mix well.

  12. Simmer partially covered until sauce thickens and traces of fat are visible. Proceed with next steps meanwhile.

  13. Pan fry chicken. Add 1 tbsp butter and place chicken. Fry on a medium heat for 2 mins. Flip.

  14. Cook until moisture evaporates or to preferred brownness. Avoid drying out chicken.

  15. Add chicken to sauce. Season and serve.
