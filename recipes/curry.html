Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken (also known as Murgh Makhani), is a rich and creamy dish featuring marinated chicken cooked in a gravy-like sauce. It's often served with rice or bread, like naan.

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 1.1 lbs boneless chicken
  • 3⁄4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1⁄3 tsp salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1⁄8 tsp turmeric
  • 1⁄2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3⁄4 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1⁄3 cup greek yogurt

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3⁄4 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 to 2 deseeded green chilis
  • 1 to 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1⁄2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1⁄2 tbsp kasuri methi
  • 1 cinnamon
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 4 cloves

Puree

  • 1.2 lbs diced tomatos
  • 28 cashews
